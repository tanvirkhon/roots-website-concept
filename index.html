Stories are our Roots

Regenerating Our Offspring Through Stories

Our mission is to use the art of storytelling to promote literacy, in order to reach the various types of learners in a multicultural society. We encourage youth to find their voices through theatre classes, monologues, etc. and inspire our seniors to share their precious memories through stories.
